Civil Rights Movement

Fighting for racial equality and justice in the United States.

Key Events

  • 1954 - Brown v. Board of Education Supreme Court ruling.

  • 1963 - March on Washington and Martin Luther King's "I Have a Dream" speech.

  • 1964 - Civil Rights Act passed, banning racial discrimination.

Famous Leaders

Martin Luther King Jr.

Leader of the civil rights movement, known for nonviolent resistance and his "I Have a Dream" speech.

Rosa Parks

Her refusal to give up her bus seat sparked the Montgomery Bus Boycott.

Malcolm X

Advocate for Black empowerment and self-defense, key figure in the Nation of Islam.
